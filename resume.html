Tufts Mail Project

When I started to notice more complaints from friends that they have to walk uphill for 20 minutes to get to the only mail service room on Tufts campus to then to stand in line for another 5 to 10 minutes to get their packages, I realized there is a challenge. It appeared that the current process of retrieving mail packages was inefficient. After consulting with several friends who had worked at mail services, I developed an app this summer that allows users to select an estimated time block during which they planned to go to mail services. The app then saves the data into Firebase, and calculates how many other people have registered for the same time interval. Based on those results, the app will suggest a better time, with a shorter waiting period, if the initial time slot selected time is filled with more than 20 people.

  • Developed iOS app aiming to solve overcrowding problem at Tufts Mail Services
  • Implemented user authentication and real time database with Firebase
  • Gained experience in auto-layout, Swift, and Xcode
